Sales Forecasting

Retail Inventory Optimization

The Challenge

A regional retail chain struggled with overstocking and stockouts due to inaccurate manual demand planning. They needed a predictive system to forecast sales at the SKU level across 50+ stores.

The Solution

I implemented a hybrid forecasting system combining statistical methods with deep learning to capture seasonality and complex trends:

  • Data Pipeline: Aggregated sales history, weather data, and holiday calendars into a unified warehouse.
  • Hybrid Model: Ensembled ARIMA for linear trends with LSTM (Long Short-Term Memory) networks to capture non-linear patterns.
  • Auto-Retraining: Implemented MLOps pipelines to automatically retrain models on new weekly data.

Technical Architecture

  • Languages: Python, SQL
  • ML Libraries: TensorFlow, Statsmodels, Prophet
  • Orchestration: Airflow for pipeline management
  • Cloud: AWS SageMaker for training and deployment

The Result

The solution predicted monthly sales with 92% accuracy, leading to a 15% reduction in inventory holding costs and a 10% increase in availability for high-demand items.
